🎯 Challenge Objective

Navigate Salty Jewellery through turbulent times and growth opportunities! In this immersive simulation, you'll face real-world business crises and develop expansion strategies. Learn to pivot during challenges and scale sustainably, ensuring Salty's affordable luxury brand thrives in India's dynamic fashion market.

🛡️ The Strategy Simulator

Experience interactive scenarios that test your decision-making skills. From supply chain disruptions to market expansion, this tool provides data-driven insights, risk assessments, and strategic recommendations tailored to Salty's Gen Z-focused business model.

🔥 Dual Challenges

Tackle these interconnected challenges to build resilient leadership skills:

  • Crisis Mode: Respond to unexpected disruptions like economic downturns, competitor threats, or supply issues affecting Salty's operations.
  • Expansion Challenge: Develop strategies for growth, including new markets, product lines, or partnerships while maintaining brand affordability and quality.

📋 Steps to Strategic Mastery

  • Enter the Salty Business Challenges simulator embedded below.
  • Begin with Crisis Mode to handle immediate threats.
  • Transition to Expansion Challenge to plan for growth post-crisis.
  • Make decisions based on provided data, budgets, and market trends.
  • Review outcomes, export strategy reports, and reflect on trade-offs.
  • Take screenshots of your crisis responses and expansion plans.
  • Submit your work and discuss lessons in the forum.

🏆 Challenge Rewards & Outputs

  • Resilience Badge: Strategic Leader certification for your portfolio.
  • Exportable Plans: Crisis response and expansion strategy documents (PDF/PNG).
  • Skill Building: Crisis management, risk assessment, and scaling strategies for fashion startups.

💎 Why This Matters for Salty

In the volatile fashion industry, Salty must balance crisis resilience with ambitious growth. This challenge equips you with tools to protect and expand the brand, ensuring trendy, affordable jewellery reaches more Gen Z customers across India and beyond.
