Quick Commerce & Delivery Excellence

How Quick Commerce Changed Salty’s Same-Day Delivery?

 

How do we put our product on quick commerce like Zepto, Instamart, or Blinkit?

 

 
Step 1
Select and Validate Your Product Category

Confirm that your products fit the quick commerce demand, typically fast-moving, essential, or trending items like fashion accessories or jewellery.

 
Step 2
Register as a Seller on the Platform

Visit the quick commerce platform’s seller registration portal and fill out the necessary forms with business and product details.

 
Step 3
Submit Required Documents

Upload essential documents such as business license, GST registration, identity proofs, bank details, and any brand or trademark certifications as required.

 
Step 4
Undergo the Verification Process

The platform will review your submitted documents and verify the credibility and compliance of your business.

 
Step 5
Integrate Product Inventory

Provide your inventory data, product catalog, pricing, and images according to the platform’s specifications. Some platforms may require you to sync inventory via APIs or upload spreadsheets.

 
Step 6
Supply Inventory to Dark Stores or Fulfillment Centers

Deliver stock to logistics-enabled dark stores/warehouses strategically located to ensure quick order processing and last-mile delivery.

 
Step 7
List and Optimize Product Display

Work with the platform to list your products effectively with optimized titles, descriptions, and images to improve visibility and conversion.

 
Step 8
Launch and Manage Orders

Once live, monitor orders through the platform dashboard, ensuring timely fulfillment, packaging, and dispatch for ultra-fast delivery.

 
Step 9
Maintain Inventory and Performance Metrics

Keep inventory updated and maintain high ratings by ensuring quality, fast shipping, and good customer service.
