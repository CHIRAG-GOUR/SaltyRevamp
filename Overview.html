Internship
 

Welcome to the Internship

 

Welcome, aspiring fashion innovators! Join the Skillizee X Salty Virtual Internship to dive into the world of Salty Jewellery, India’s trendsetting D2C brand redefining style with bold, affordable, and high-quality accessories.

Real-world project experience: Interns work on tasks that mirror real operations, marketing, creative, or strategy roles.

What You Will Learn

 

Explore the art of building a modern jewellery brand with hands-on insights:

  • Brand Story: Discover how Salty evolved from an idea to a fashion-forward brand.
  • Mission and Vision: Learn how Salty’s core values shape its identity and connect with customers.
  • Product Innovation: Understand the creation of trendy accessories like necklaces, earrings, and bag charms.
  • Marketing Strategies: Master social media, SEO, and influencer strategies to engage Gen Z audiences.
  • Business Operations: Gain insights into pricing, supply chain, and the Business Model Canvas.
  • Hands-On Experience: Create branding, marketing, and strategic materials to reflect Salty’s vision.

Quizzes, Assignments and Projects

As you progress, demonstrate your mastery to gain certificate these are required to be completed:

  • Quizzes: Achieve a passing grade of 75% to unlock the next module.
  • Assignments: Earn a passing grade of 60% to showcase your creativity.
  • Capstone Project: Complete the final capstone project and apply all learned concepts.
