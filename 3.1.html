All in Capital

As a lead investor in Salty’s seed funding round, All in Capital provided significant financial support to fuel the company’s early growth.

  • Contributed to the ₹5–₹5.4 crore seed funding round in Early 2024.
  • Offered strategic guidance in scaling Salty’s e-commerce operations.
  • Connected Salty with key industry players in the fashion and jewelry market.
