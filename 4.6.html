Chapter 3: GEO, SEO & Organic Growth Strategy

Understanding GEO and SEO for Salty’s digital presence

What Is GEO and Why It Is Important in 2025

GEO stands for generative engine optimization. Think of it as SEO's evolution for the AI age. SEO gets you ranked while GEO gets you quoted. The term is starting to gain traction with SEO experts and marketers to differentiate between making your content more visible to AI and making your links rank high on a search page. You still need SEO, but now it's going to be complemented by GEO, which can open up your content to a whole new audience.

Key Differences Between SEO and GEO

Additional Notes

  • SEO elements like alt tags and hashtags help search engines recognize content, but narrative style is not necessary for GEO.
  • GEO content must demonstrate expertise and trustworthiness to be used as a resource by AI models.
  • SEO targets entire pages or sites, while GEO focuses on making specific content easily extractable and understandable without full page context.
  • Writing content in clear Q&A formats supports GEO by matching user queries with clear AI-extractable answers.
