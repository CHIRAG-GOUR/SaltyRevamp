Business Canvas Quest

Learn Business Model Canvas with Salty

?? Quest Objective

Embark on an interactive journey to master the Business Model Canvas (BMC) framework! In this quest, you'll first learn the fundamentals through an engaging tutorial, then apply your knowledge to analyze Salty Jewellery's real-world business model. Complete both levels to unlock your Business Canvas certificate and gain strategic insights for trendy fashion startups.

?? The Quest Structure

The Business Canvas Quest consists of two progressive levels designed to build your skills step-by-step:

  • Level 1: BMC Tutorial - Discover the 9 building blocks of the BMC with interactive explanations, examples, and quick quizzes.
  • Level 2: Salty's BMC - Apply the framework to Salty Jewellery's operations, filling in the canvas with real business data and reflecting on strategic decisions.

?? Steps to Conquer the Quest

  • Start with Level 1 to build foundational knowledge.
  • Progress to Level 2 once comfortable with the basics.
  • Interact with the embedded tool, complete all sections, and save your progress.
  • Take screenshots or export your completed canvases as evidence of completion.
  • Reflect on key learnings in the discussion forum for peer insights.

?? Quest Rewards & Outputs

  • Certificate: Digital badge for completing both levels.
  • Portfolio Piece: Exportable BMC canvases (PDF/PNG) for your resume.
  • Skill Building: Master BMC for business planning, suitable for Salty's internship applications.

?? Why This Quest Matters for Salty

Understanding the BMC empowers you to think like a founder! Salty Jewellery uses this framework to strategize affordable luxury accessories for Gen Z. By completing this quest, you'll gain tools to innovate in the competitive fashion industry.
