Personalized & Themed Collections

Naming Collections & Creating Themes

Personalized & Themed Collections: Salty often names collections to reflect emotion, personality, or moments, like “Round Memories Necklace,” “Forever Us Pendant,” “Cursive Heart Charm,” or “11:11 Necklace.” Each collection or product name is chosen to evoke a particular feeling or to help customers project their own narrative onto the piece. Bespoke and personalised items play into storytelling by allowing buyers to have names or initials engraved, making each piece part of a personal or gifting story.

Festive & Theme-Based Drops

Seasonal & Theme-Based Drops

Besides the expansion into new product lines, SALTY also continues to curate exclusive festive collections like Rakhi gifts. With Diwali around the corner in October, the brand is gearing up to launch a special range of gifts and fashion accessories perfectly suited for the festive season, blending traditional charm with contemporary style.

Emotional Hooks: Collection descriptions typically focus on sentiments (“Golden Love Minimal,” “Velvet Name Pendant”), making the jewellery an emblem of memories, love, or a unique style journey.

Social Media Campaigns

Social Media Campaigns & Instagram Aesthetics

Jewellery in Real-Life Moments: Instagram and other social platforms are used to share not just product shots, but curated feeds and reels that show jewellery in real-life moments—on diverse models, with playful “unboxing” videos, and through stories that highlight customer inspiration and behind-the-scenes creation.

Consistent Aesthetic: The brand’s IG feed is full of rich, consistent colors and minimal yet bold styling, establishing an instantly recognizable mood as part of Salty’s digital identity.

Influencer & User Stories: Stories, reels, and featured influencer collaborations help project a lifestyle or aspirational story—making customers imagine themselves as part of that narrative, rather than simply buying a product.

Interactive Campaigns: Salty leverages interactive campaigns, like challenges, polls, or collaborations with micro-influencers, to get customers to participate in the storytelling journey and share their experiences.
