Expansion into New Categories

Expansion into New Categories

We envision Salty evolving into a comprehensive accessories brand, expanding beyond jewellery to categories such as eyewear, belts, bags, and hats. Our focus remains on delivering innovative, trend-forward offerings while maintaining accessibility and inclusivity.

Investment in Talent and Technology

Investment in Talent and Technology

To support growth, Salty intends to invest in recruiting for critical roles across design, marketing, operations, and technology teams to build capacity and accelerate innovation. The company is launching a dedicated mobile app to enhance the customer experience, enable faster shopping, and strengthen community engagement through personalized outreach and promotions.

Commitment to Innovation and Partnerships

Commitment to Innovation and Partnerships

The brand is focused on relentless innovation and customer satisfaction while exploring new collaborations and partnerships to fuel growth and market reach. While specific timelines are under consideration, our commitment to delivering trend-forward offerings remains unwavering.

 
Vision as a Lifestyle Brand

Vision as a Lifestyle Brand

Overall, Salty is rapidly growing from a niche D2C fashion jewellery startup to a broader lifestyle accessory player targeting India’s youth and emerging as a key competitor in the affordable fashion accessories market.
