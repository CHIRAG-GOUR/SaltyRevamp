From Idea to Reality

Salty's Essence

What does the brand stand for?

Salty Jewellery stands for making high-quality, trendy, and affordable accessories accessible to everyone, while celebrating individuality and contemporary style. The brand’s mission is to be India’s trusted go-to destination for fashion jewellery, offering pieces that give every customer a unique edge and confidence in their look.

Salty is about “stepping up your style” and celebrating confidence, personality, and fashion freedom. It stands for reliable quality and trendy, accessible pieces that match every mood, event, and individual preference.

Culture Meets Gen Z

How does it connect with modern youth and cultural identity?

Our journey stems from an analytical exploration of e-commerce opportunities. After recognizing the burgeoning demand for accessories, particularly jewellery, we set out to establish a brand that offers quality and frequent design updates. Our product range spans earrings, necklaces, rings, bracelets, and jewellery sets, catering to the dynamic preferences of our audience.

Salty Jewellery Timeline (2022–2025)
Click on the following years to know about Salty

2022

  • Founded: Salty is launched in New Delhi by Sonaal Goel, Twishaa Gupta, and Kanishka Garg, targeting Gen Z and young urban consumers with trendy, affordable, high-quality fashion jewellery.
  • Mission: To make expressive, contemporary accessories widely accessible through digital-first retail and K-style/Western-inspired designs.

2023

  • Expansion: Salty Jewellery grows its online presence with a revamped e-commerce platform, introducing new collections inspired by global fashion trends.
  • Partnerships: Collaborates with local artisans to create unique, culturally inspired designs, strengthening community ties.

Early 2024

  • Innovation: Launches a customization feature allowing customers to personalize jewellery, enhancing individual expression.
  • Recognition: Salty receives an award for 'Best Emerging Fashion Jewellery Brand' in India.

2025

  • Global Reach: Salty expands internationally, targeting markets in Southeast Asia and the Middle East.
  • Sustainability: Introduces eco-friendly packaging and sustainable materials in select collections.
