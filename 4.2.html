Meta Ads and Google Ads

What are Meta Ads and Google Ads?

Meta Ads are advertising solutions offered across Meta’s platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and the Audience Network. They focus heavily on visual, interest-based, and demographic targeting to build brand awareness, engagement, and social proof through image, video, carousel, story, and reel formats.

Google Ads is a pay-per-click advertising platform across Google Search, YouTube, Gmail, and Google Display Network. It targets users based on search intent using keywords, allowing ads to appear in search results, videos, display banners, and shopping ads.

Salty’s Advertising Strategy

Salty’s Strategy with Meta and Google Ads

Meta Ads:

  • Salty leverages Meta’s strength in building brand awareness and engaging younger demographics via visually appealing ads on Instagram and Facebook.
  • Influencer-generated content is often boosted through Meta campaigns to maximize social proof and audience engagement.
  • Campaigns use detailed audience segmentation (interest, location, behavior) to target their fashion-conscious young consumer base.
  • Live shopping events and story ads deepen engagement and drive impulse purchases.

Google Ads:

  • Salty uses Google Ads to capture high-intent audiences actively searching for trendy and affordable jewellery.
  • Search ads, Shopping ads, and YouTube video ads help convert demand into sales with keyword-focused campaigns.
  • Google Ads complement Meta by targeting customers already in the consideration or purchase phase.
  • Data from Google Ads is used to refine targeting and improve ROI with smart bidding strategies.
