Chapter 2: Creativity in Action: From Trends to Design

Crafting Jewellery with Global Inspiration

Where Ideas Begin

How new collections are imagined

Salty’s design inspiration is a creative fusion of art, culture, lifestyle, and youth trends. The design team collaborates closely with marketing experts to analyze emerging fashion trends and cultural shifts. This ensures their collections resonate with the fast-evolving preferences of modern consumers who are digitally connected and style-savvy. Designers draw from a wide palette of influences including K-style fashion, Western trends, street culture, and youth aspirations to create fresh, statement-worthy jewellery pieces that are both fashionable and wearable.

Art You Can Wear

“Jewellery as wearable art”

Salty views jewellery as more than just adornment; it is wearable art that combines craftsmanship, creativity, and personal expression. Each piece is carefully designed to be visually captivating while serving as a medium of storytelling and identity for the wearer. This concept embraces the idea that jewellery reflects individual style, cultural narratives, and emotion, transforming everyday accessories into pieces of art that carry meaning and character.
