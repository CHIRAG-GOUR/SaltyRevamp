Chapter 2: Funding, Expansion & Technology Investments

Fueling Salty’s Growth

Seed Funding Success

Seed Funding Success

After bootstrapping profitably for the initial year, Salty raised Rs 5.4 crore in a Seed round in January 2024, led by All in Capital and Anicut Capital, with participation from Suashish Diamonds, JK Group, and other angel investors. This funding, a transformative milestone, supports team expansion, product diversification, and technological advancements.

Impact of Funding

Impact of Funding

  • Team Expansion: Recruiting for critical roles in design, marketing, operations, and technology to enhance innovation and scaling.
  • Product Portfolio Growth: Expanding from 1,000 to over 3,000 SKUs to cater to a wider market with frequent new launches.
  • Technological Investments: Launching a dedicated mobile app to improve customer experience and foster loyalty.
  •  
  • Marketing & Market Expansion: Increased budgets for digital marketing, influencer partnerships, and quick commerce to boost visibility and customer acquisition in tier 2 and tier 3 cities.
  • Operational Scaling: Strengthening supply chain, inventory control, and backend infrastructure to support a Rs 40 crore ARR in 2024.
Technology Investments for Growth

Technology Investments for Growth

An imitation jewellery brand can invest in technology for growth through several strategic channels:

  1. E-commerce Platform: Develop a user-friendly, mobile-optimized website or app to reach a wider audience and provide a seamless shopping experience with easy navigation, personalized recommendations, and secure payment gateways.
  2. Inventory and Supply Chain Management: Use automated inventory tracking, demand forecasting, and supplier management software to optimize stock levels, reduce dead stock, and speed up turnaround time.
  3. Digital Marketing and SEO: Invest in SEO tools and digital marketing technologies to increase online visibility, attract organic traffic, and compete with larger marketplaces.
  4. Data Analytics and Customer Insights: Leverage customer data platforms and analytics to understand buying patterns, customer preferences, and market trends for targeted product launches and promotions.
  5. Social Media and Influencer Marketing Tools: Utilize platforms that help identify and manage influencer collaborations, monitor social media engagement, and run targeted ad campaigns.
  6. Quality Control and Testing Technologies: Implement technologies for material testing and quality assurance to maintain product standards and build customer trust.
  7. Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Try-Ons: Integrate AR tools that allow customers to virtually try on jewellery, enhancing online shopping confidence.
  8. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Systems: Automate customer interactions from inquiries to post-sale support for better service and retention.

By focusing on these technology investments, an imitation jewellery brand can boost operational efficiency, build a stronger digital presence, deliver better customer experiences, and scale sustainably in a competitive market.
