Founder’s of Salty

Founder’s Story and Brand Origins

 

Salty, an emerging fashion accessories brand, was introduced by ambitious entrepreneurs – Twishaa Gupta, Sonaal Goel, and Kanishka Garg – alumni of prestigious institutions like LSR, ISB, and SRCC. Their strategic analysis of India’s e-commerce space revealed a gap for high-quality, frequently-updated fashion accessories. Inspired by Instagram sellers and the online shopping boom, they aimed to build a national brand blending creativity and reliability.

Behind the Name: Salty

Why the Name “Salty”?

 

The name “Salty” reflects freshness, boldness, and a youthful identity—bringing “flavour” to fashion. Just as salt is essential and adds character to food, Salty Jewellery seeks to be essential in modern style, offering unique pieces that stand out.

The Idea That Drove Us

Inspiration Behind The Brand Concept

 

Inspired to create an “Etsy for India,” Salty makes trendy, high-quality jewellery accessible. Their collections balance creativity, market trends, and affordability. Weekly design updates, collaborations, and durable materials like brass, copper alloy, and stainless steel with anti-tarnish techniques keep Salty stylish and long-lasting.
