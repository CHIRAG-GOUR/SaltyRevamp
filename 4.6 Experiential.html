Ads Manager Simulator

Learn Digital Advertising with Salty

?? Mission Objective

Dive into the world of Meta advertising! In this simulator, you'll master the Meta Ads Manager dashboard by creating, launching, and optimizing ad campaigns for Salty Jewellery's trendy accessories. Target Gen Z urban consumers in India and learn to drive engagement, conversions, and ROI through hands-on practice.

??? The Simulator Experience

Experience a realistic Meta Ads Manager interface with guided challenges that mirror real-world advertising scenarios. No prior experience needed – the simulator provides tooltips, best practices, and instant feedback to build your digital marketing skills.

?? Campaign Challenges

Complete these sequential challenges to unlock the full power of Meta advertising:

  • Challenge 1: Campaign Setup - Create your first ad campaign, select objectives, and define targeting for Salty's new collection.
  • Challenge 2: Ad Creative & Budget - Design compelling ad creatives, set budgets, and schedule your campaign launch.
  • Challenge 3: Optimization & Reporting - Monitor performance metrics, A/B test variations, and optimize for maximum ROI.

?? Steps to Launch Your Ads

  • Access the Meta Ad Playground simulator embedded below.
  • Start with Challenge 1 and progress through all three challenges.
  • Follow in-simulator prompts to configure campaigns using Salty Jewellery's branding and audience data.
  • Review performance reports and export your campaign summaries.
  • Take screenshots of your completed campaigns and dashboard views.
  • Submit your work and reflect on strategies in the discussion forum.

?? Simulator Rewards & Outputs

  • Digital Badge: Meta Ads Specialist certification for your portfolio.
  • Exportable Reports: Campaign summaries and performance dashboards (PDF/PNG).
  • Skill Mastery: Hands-on experience with targeting, budgeting, and analytics for fashion marketing.

?? Why Meta Ads for Salty?

Meta platforms (Facebook & Instagram) are perfect for Salty's Gen Z audience. This simulator teaches you to craft scroll-stopping ads that showcase affordable luxury jewellery, driving viral engagement and sales in India's competitive fashion market.
