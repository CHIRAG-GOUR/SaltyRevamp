Chapter 4: Brand Identity & Customer Experience

Salty: Where Buying Meets "Experience"

Packaging and Unboxing

Packaging and Unboxing as an “Experience”

Stylish, Branded Packaging: Every Salty piece comes in chic, custom-branded boxes or micro-fiber pouches, making each order feel like an exclusive gift. Packaging is designed to reflect youthful, trendy aesthetics and protect the jewellery inside.

Unboxing Delight: The thoughtful packaging, often with care instructions or thank-you notes, turns the unboxing into a share-worthy moment that customers frequently feature on social media, boosting organic visibility and reinforcing the brand’s premium yet approachable vibe.

Buyer Journey

Buyer Journey: Discovery → Purchase → Sharing Online

  • Discovery: Customers discover Salty mainly through engaging social media content, regular product drops, influencer partnerships, and search-friendly, visually appealing online campaigns.
  • Purchase: The buying process on salty.co.in is designed to be simple and secure, with detailed product info, multiple payment options (including COD), and a hassle-free 7-day return/exchange policy for peace of mind.
  • Sharing Online: After purchase, many customers share their Salty unboxing experiences, styling photos, or reviews on Instagram and other platforms using branded hashtags. Salty encourages this by resharing user content, further growing its community and reinforcing the “Salty experience” across digital touchpoints.
Customer Engagement

Driving Loyalty and Advocacy

Salty’s focus on experience—before, during, and after purchase—transforms ordinary shopping into an engaging lifestyle event, driving both loyalty and brand advocacy among young, social-first customers.
