Price Point Strategy: Affordable Luxury

Salty Jewellery follows a strategy of affordable luxury, offering trendy and high-quality fashion jewellery at accessible price points that appeal to young consumers. Prices typically range from about ?400 to ?2,500, allowing customers to buy stylish, well-crafted pieces without overspending. This balance helps make Salty attractive to students, young professionals, and fashion-conscious buyers who want fashionable jewellery that looks premium but is budget-friendly.

The brand emphasizes combining style, durability, and quality materials to give customers value for money, promoting the idea that luxury should be inclusive and attainable. Various affordable combos and sets are also available, encouraging customers to experiment with different looks while staying within budget.
