Chapter 3: Demographic, Community, and Connections

How Salty Targets and Engages Its Youthful Audience

Target Demographic

Insight into Salty's Target Demographic

Our target demographic primarily comprises individuals aged 18 to 35, with a focus on tier 2 and tier 3 cities. We've observed significant traction from both men and women, reflecting the inclusivity of our offerings. We've garnered a diverse customer base spanning various regions and demographics by offering a blend of affordability and on-trend designs. We have good demand from the North East. We get orders from Jammu and Kashmir as well as from Western India, and that's because we get good responses from the blend of price points and designs we offer.

Salty Jewellery sells across India with strong presence in:

  • North India: Especially Delhi (HQ) and Jammu & Kashmir
  • West India: Maharashtra and Gujarat
  • South & Northeast India: Rapidly growing order volumes
  • Tier 2 & Tier 3 Cities: Major focus to reach Gen Z beyond metros
Building a Youth-Oriented Community

Building a Youth-Oriented Community

Access and Inclusion: Salty positions itself as a brand for the young and style-conscious, making fashion jewellery affordable and trendy for both women and men across India.

Community Engagement: Regular product drops, interactive social media content, and participative campaigns invite young consumers to be part of Salty's style revolution, fostering a sense of belonging and shared fashion identity.

Empowerment: By supporting authentic influencer voices, user stories, and feedback-driven design, Salty empowers its community to express individuality and co-create trends.

Collaboration with Creators

Collaboration with Creators

Diverse Collaboration Programs: Salty actively invites partnerships in gifting, discount codes, affiliate marketing, content co-creation, campaign participation, and more—enabling influencers and creators to collaborate based on their strengths.

Amplifying Reach: Influencers and micro-creators help Salty connect with niche communities and youth audiences, driving word-of-mouth and social buzz for new collections.

Content Creation: Creators work with Salty on styling videos, product reviews, social challenges, and campaign shoots, helping build a dynamic and visually compelling brand identity.

Brand Campaigns & Events: Salty occasionally partners for limited-edition launches, events, or pop-ups that create additional buzz and exclusivity, further powering digital marketing impact.

Key Elements of Salty's Influencer and Meta Connection

Key Elements of Salty's Influencer and Meta Connection

Selecting Relevant Influencers: Collaborate with fashion, beauty, and lifestyle influencers whose followers align with Salty's ideal customers. Micro-influencers (10K-50K followers) are often targeted for higher engagement and niche audience reach.

Content Creation: Influencers create styling guides, tutorials, and authentic posts wearing Salty jewellery, showcasing versatility and quality.


Utilizing Meta Ads and Analytics: Salty uses Meta Business Suite to run targeted Facebook and Instagram ads, retargeting website visitors and optimizing ad creatives based on data insights. Influencer-generated content is repurposed for paid campaigns to amplify reach.

Community Building and Social Proof: Salty encourages followers to share user-generated content wearing Salty jewellery, creating a loyal community and authentic social proof that drives trust and conversions.

This approach positions Salty as a vibrant, youthful community where creators and customers collaborate to shape fashion, fostering long-lasting loyalty and engagement with the brand.
